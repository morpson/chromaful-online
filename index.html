Engine Features

Procedural wallpaper generation algorithms, customizable with direct canvas shaders.

11+ Procedural Algorithms

Choose from solid, linear, radial, bilinear, plasma, conic, twisted, flow field, voronoi patterns, stripes, and isolines.

6-Color Auto-Palette Builder

Select a single color, click one button, and generate a beautiful, harmonized 6-color gradient using customized HSL-shifting formulas.

Smart Dark Overlay ("Darkify")

Mute overly bright colors into a deep indigo-vignette dark backdrop. Built-in canvas multiply filters preserve colorful highlights.

Live Drift Animation

Breathe life into static backgrounds. Gpu-accelerated rendering shifts coordinate offsets fluidly. Control movement speed or freeze in place.

Tactile Film Grain

Overlay a hardware-accelerated film texture layer. Add grain structure variables to give gradients a warm, tactile paper feel.

Multi-Resolution 4K Downloads

Export high-fidelity, uncompressed PNG files customized to match your monitors. Supports 1080p, 1440p, ultra-wide, and 4K UHD.
